1. **Visit website** → Find privacy policy link (usually footer)
2. **Extract policy text** (PDF or HTML)
3. **Classify policy age** (last updated date extraction)
4. **Tokenize policy** for compliance analysis

**Phase 3: Compliance Gap Analysis**

Run identified policy against Artemis compliance engine:

**APP 5 (Notification) Audit:**
- Scan for: "financial institution", "third party", "disclosure", "bank"

- Flag severity: HIGH

- Scan for: "encryption", "access controls", "data breach", "incident response"
- Policies missing this: 64-72% of sector
- Flag severity: MEDIUM-HIGH

**APP 1 (Management) Audit:**
- Scan for: "data retention", "data deletion", "storage location", "responsibility"
