## THE STRATEGY (In 30 Seconds)

**Email 1 (Day 0): The Gift**
- Subject: "We found a gap in [Company]'s privacy policy"
- Opens with their specific compliance gap
- Proves value by fixing one gap for free

- Tone: "I noticed something, here's proof I can help"
- No hard sell

**Email 2 (Day 4): The Deadline**

- Tone: "Here's why this matters, and why now"

**Email 3 (Day 10): The Peer Pressure**
- Subject: "X agencies have already reviewed Privacy Act compliance"
- Follows up on Email 2 (sent only to people who opened/clicked it)
- Social proof + early-mover advantage
- Soft CTA: free checklist (no cost, no hard sell)
- Tone: "Competitors are moving, you should too"

**Result:** 3-6% of people who get Email 1 purchase the A$149 report. Some upgrade to A$499 policy draft.

## ARTEMIS SETUP (15 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create Campaign (2 minutes)
1. Log into Artemis
2. Create new campaign: "PolicyCheck_RealEstate_April2026"
3. Select list: Real estate agency emails (validated)
4. Naming convention: [Product]_[Sector]_[Month][Year]

### Step 2: Add Email 1 (3 minutes)
1. Copy entire Email 1 template from `emails-real-estate.md`
2. Paste into Artemis Email 1 field
3. Set send date: Day 0 (today or tomorrow)
4. Set send time: 9:00 AM AEST
5. Use subject line variant from `subject-lines.md` (Real Estate Email 1)
6. Verify all personalisation fields are set: [First Name], [Agency Name], [Phone], [Address]
7. Verify unsubscribe link works
8. **DO NOT SEND YET** (wait for compliance check)

### Step 3: Add Email 2 (3 minutes)
1. Copy Email 2 template from `emails-real-estate.md`
2. Paste into Artemis Email 2 field
3. Set conditional send: "Only to Email 1 openers"
4. Set send date: Day 4 (4 days after Email 1)
5. Set send time: 9:00 AM AEST
6. Use subject line variant from `subject-lines.md` (Real Estate Email 2)
7. Verify all fields match Email 1

### Step 4: Add Email 3 (3 minutes)
1. Copy Email 3 template from `emails-real-estate.md`
2. Paste into Artemis Email 3 field
3. Set conditional send: "Only to Email 2 openers/clickers"
4. Set send date: Day 10 (6 days after Email 2)
5. Set send time: 9:00 AM AEST
6. Use subject line variant from `subject-lines.md` (Real Estate Email 3)

### Step 5: Compliance Check (4 minutes)
Before sending, verify (from `email-compliance-checklist.md`):
- [ ] Sender name is real person, not "Team"
- [ ] Sender phone is business line, not mobile
- [ ] Sender address is complete (Street, Suburb, State, Postcode)
- [ ] All subject lines are truthful (not misleading)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link is working and visible in footer
- [ ] No hard bounces in list (validate emails first)

**If all checked: Ready to launch**

2. **Export as CSV** with columns: First Name, Last Name, Email, Company Name
3. **Remove existing customers** (don't email people who already bought)
4. **Remove existing unsubscribes** (don't email people who opted out previously)

**Quick validation in Excel/Google Sheets:**
- Remove any row with email that doesn't have @ symbol
- Remove any row with email that ends in .test or .dev

## TROUBLESHOOTING (If Something Goes Wrong)

### Email bounces are high (>10%)
- **Problem:** List has bad emails
- **Solution:** Validate list against source directory before sending
- **Action:** Remove bouncing emails, research valid emails, retry
- **Time to fix:** 2-3 hours

### Open rate is low (<25%)
- **Problem:** Subject lines aren't compelling or emails hitting spam folder
- **Solution:** (a) Try different subject line variant, or (b) check domain reputation
- **Action:** Switch to curiosity or urgency variant from subject-lines.md
- **Time to fix:** Send test to 100 people with variant, measure open rate, then scale

### Click rate is low (<5% on Email 2)
- **Problem:** A$149 price feels expensive or value isn't clear
- **Solution:** Add more specific value in Email 2 (e.g., "shows exactly which of the 13 APPs you're missing")
- **Action:** Edit Email 2 before sending to next sector
- **Time to fix:** 30 minutes

### Replies aren't being handled
- **Problem:** Sales team isn't following up within 24 hours
- **Solution:** Brief sales on SLA (hot leads = 4 hours, warm leads = 24 hours)
- **Action:** Set daily 9 AM standup to review replies
- **Time to fix:** 1 meeting

### People are unsubscribing (>2% rate)
- **Problem:** Subject lines are misleading or Email 1 overpromises
- **Solution:** Review unsubscribe feedback, adjust messaging
- **Action:** If "not relevant" feedback, list quality issue. If "too salesy," soften tone
- **Time to fix:** 1-2 hours per email adjustment
