### Pre-Launch Checklist

1. **Artemis Configuration**
   - [ ] Create campaign for each sector (Real Estate, Chemists, Lawyers)
   - [ ] Paste Email 1 template into Artemis, using subject line variants from subject-lines.md
   - [ ] Verify all emails include sender name, phone, business address (per compliance checklist)
   - [ ] Set Email 1 send date/time: Day 0, 9:00 AM AEST
   - [ ] Set Email 2 to send only to Email 1 openers on Day 4, 9:00 AM AEST
   - [ ] Set Email 3 to send only to Email 2 openers/clickers on Day 10, 9:00 AM AEST
   - [ ] Enable unsubscribe link and verify it routes to preference center
   - [ ] Enable bounce handling (auto-retry soft bounces, flag hard bounces)
   - [ ] Enable reply tracking and route to sales@policycheck.au

2. **List Preparation**
   - [ ] Real Estate list: Use Real Estate Institute directory + LinkedIn property agents

   - [ ] Lawyer list: Use Law Society directory + conveyancing networks
   - [ ] Validate all emails (remove bounces from prior campaigns)
   - [ ] Remove existing customers from list
   - [ ] Remove existing unsubscribes from list

   - [ ] Personalisation fields: [First Name], [Agency Name], [Pharmacy Name], [Law Firm Name], [Phone], [Address]
   - [ ] Links: [TEASER_REPORT_LINK], [FULL_REPORT_UNLOCK_LINK], [FREE_CHECKLIST_LINK] must be valid

   - [ ] Sender phone: Use main business line
   - [ ] Sender address: Use registered business address

4. **Internal Team Alignment**
   - [ ] Sales team briefed on warm lead handling (see cadence-rules.md)

   - [ ] Compliance team scheduled for weekly reviews (Tuesday 10 AM AEST)

   - [ ] All emails reviewed against compliance checklist (email-compliance-checklist.md)

   - [ ] Email footers include all required sender info

## TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### If Open Rate is Below Target

**Real Estate:** Below 35%
- Check: Subject line clarity (are people getting the teaser?)
- Try: Swap to more curiosity-focused variants (subjects 1-3 in subject-lines.md)
- Review: Are emails hitting inbox or spam folder? Check domain reputation.

**Chemists:** Below 38%
- Check: Subject line clarity (are emails being opened?)
- Try: Swap to authority-focused variants (subjects 16-20 in subject-lines.md)
- Review: Are you hitting decision-maker (pharmacist) or receptionist? Try both.

**Lawyers:** Below 32%
- Check: Are emails hitting partners' inboxes? They may be filtering marketing.
- Try: Swap to professional-tone variants (subjects 31-35 in subject-lines.md)
- Review: Consider phone outreach instead of email for lawyers.

### If Click Rate is Below Target

**All sectors:** Below target CTR for Email 2
- Likely issue: Email 2 offer (A$149 price point) is weak or unclear
- Action: Add more specific value statement in Email 2 (e.g., "shows exactly which of the 13 APPs you're missing")
- Try: Add social proof earlier in Email 2 ("247 agencies have reviewed their gaps already")

**All sectors:** Below target CTR for Email 3
- Likely issue: Free checklist isn't compelling, or people forgot the campaign by Day 10
- Action: Change Email 3 CTA (try policy draft link instead of checklist, or add "last chance" urgency)
- Try: Send Email 3 on Day 8 instead of Day 10 (while momentum is higher)

### If Bounce Rate is Above 5%

- Check list quality: Did you validate emails before importing?
- Action: Cross-reference list against source directory (Real Estate Institute, etc.)
- Solution: Remove all bouncing emails, test new list segment before full send

### If Unsubscribe Rate is Above 2%

- Check subject lines: Are they misleading? (would cause people to unsubscribe)
- Check Email 1 messaging: Did you overpromise or undersell?
- Action: Review unsubscribe emails (many systems show reason)
- Solution: Adjust messaging for next sector or retargeting wave

### If Reply Rate is Below Expected

- Check: Are replies actually being routed to sales@policycheck.au?
- Check: Is sales team actually responding within 24 hours?
- Action: Verify Artemis reply routing
- Solution: If routing is correct, replies may be low because open rate is low (see above)
