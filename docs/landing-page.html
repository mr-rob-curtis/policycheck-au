Frequently asked questions

Is this legal advice? ▼

No. PolicyCheck AU is a compliance analysis tool, not legal advice. We analyse your policy against the 13 Australian Privacy Principles and provide template language based on OAIC guidance. You should have a lawyer review your final policy, especially if your organisation handles sensitive data.

How long does the analysis take? ▼

Under 60 seconds. Our AI instantly crawls your website, extracts your privacy policy (if one exists), and compares it against the 13 Australian Privacy Principles. You'll get your compliance score and first gap immediately.

What is the July 1, 2026 deadline? ▼

From 1 July 2026, Australian businesses with an annual turnover under A$3 million lose their exemption from the Privacy Act. This means 100,000+ small businesses must now comply with the Act, including having a compliant privacy policy. Non-compliance can result in civil penalties of up to A$66,000 per contravention.

What about the December 2026 ADM rules? ▼

From December 2026, the Australian Data Metrics (ADM) framework requires eligible organisations to publish annual reports on their data handling practices. Our Enterprise tier includes an audit checklist to help you prepare for ADM compliance. Early preparation significantly reduces compliance costs.

Is my data stored when I run an analysis? ▼

No. We never store your website URL, privacy policy, or any results. Each analysis is processed once and discarded. We only retain your analysis results if you purchase a report and create an account. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I upgrade after my free analysis? ▼

Yes. Your free analysis gives you a compliance score and identifies one gap. You can then upgrade to Professional (A$149) for the full gap analysis and template language, or Enterprise (A$499) for a generated policy draft and ADM audit readiness.

What about businesses that are already compliant? ▼

Congratulations! PolicyCheck AU will give you a compliance score above 90%, and help you identify any remaining gaps to achieve 100%. You'll also get recommendations on ADM readiness and emerging Privacy Act updates.
